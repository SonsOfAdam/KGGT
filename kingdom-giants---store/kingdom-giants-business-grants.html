Kingdom Giants Grant: Raising Kingdom Financiers

Our mission is to empower Kingdom financiers—men and women committed to advancing Christ-centered businesses through strategic investments.

Kingdom Business Funding
Who Can Apply?

1. Christian entrepreneurs with business ideas aligned with Kingdom values.

2. Existing Christ-centered businesses seeking financial support to scale.

3. Innovative projects that serve both the body of Christ and the marketplace.

What We Fund

Startups: Christian entrepreneurs hoping to transform their ideas into a thriving business.
‍
‍Existing Businesses: Scaling Kingdom-driven businesses to global standards.
‍
‍Innovative Solutions: Supporting ideas that promote Kingdom impact and economic empowerment.

For Startups (New Business Ideas)

Do you have a Kingdom business idea? Let’s help you bring it to life;

1. Fill out the Kingdom Business Idea application form.

2. Submit a detailed business plan outlining your vision, mission, impact, and funding requirements.

3. Shortlisted applicants will be invited to a Kingdom Business Incubation Program, where they will receive mentorship, training, and guidance to refine their ideas.

4. Participants will pitch their ideas to the Kingdom Giants Panel, which consists of experienced Christian investors and business leaders.

5. Selected businesses will receive grants, strategic mentorship, and networking opportunities to bring their vision to life.

6. Successful entrepreneurs will also gain access to ongoing support, advisory services, and potential follow-on funding.

Get Started —Apply Now
For Existing Businesses Seeking Investment

Already running a business? Let’s help you scale!

1. Fill out the Existing Business Funding application form.

2. Provide details on revenue, growth potential, and impact.

3. Businesses that meet our funding criteria will be contacted for further evaluation.

4. Approved businesses receive financial grants, strategic mentorship, and investor connections.

5. Selected businesses may also join the Kingdom Business Accelerator Program, offering access to investor networks, business scaling strategies, and global market entry support.
