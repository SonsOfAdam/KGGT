Membership Registration Guidelines

To complete your registration, a $50 annual membership fee is required. This fee supports our mission and community initiatives.

Get Membership — in NGN Get Membership — in USD

Your annual $50 membership renewal is a direct investment in Kingdom impact. These funds go into our Community Purse, supporting small projects and providing aid to those in need, ensuring we embody God’s love through generosity and community service.

We require a community page that will feature the names and photos of registered students or members. If a member has not uploaded a picture, a default image or avatar will be used as a placeholder.

Additionally, KGG Tribe will have a blog where members can share their thoughts, review topics taught, and engage in discussions by commenting on messages they have listened to.
